Get to know me!

Hey! It's Veysi ADIN and I'm an Embedded System Engineer located in Malmö, Sweden, with over three years of experience in developing and testing software for various embedded systems. I have expertise in C, C++, and Python programming languages, as well as in hardware architectures, operating systems, and communication protocols. I worked on projects involving robotics, medical devices, and consumer electronic products, delivering high-quality, safe, and reliable software solutions. Having obtained a master's degree in robotics and control software development, I enjoy robotics, electronics, DIY projects, and learning new things every day. I like to develop applications that solve real-life problems.
